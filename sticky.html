Rabbit vs Turtle

Want to race? This rabbit vs. turtle game plots Rascal, a fast, but easily distracted hare against a frustratingly slow and methodical turtle named Timmy. For the rabbit's sake, he'd better hope that Farmer John's family doesn't want bunny stew, otherwise I'm willing to bet the turtle has a better shot than usual at winning, no matter how long he takes to complete the course!
